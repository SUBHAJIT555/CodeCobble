FAQ

What platforms do you develop apps for?

We develop apps for iOS, Android, and cross-platform solutions using technologies like React Native and Flutter. Our goal is to deliver seamless performance across all platforms.

How much time does it take to develop a mobile app?

The timeline for app development depends on the complexity and features of the app. On average, it can take anywhere from 4 to 12 weeks. A detailed project plan is provided during the consultation phase.

Do you offer post-launch support and maintenance?

Yes, we provide ongoing support and maintenance to ensure your app remains updated, secure, and optimized for user satisfaction.

Can you integrate third-party services into the app?

Absolutely! We can integrate various third-party APIs and services, such as payment gateways, social media platforms, analytics tools, and more, to enhance your app's functionality.

How much does app development cost?

The cost of app development varies depending on the app's complexity, features, and platform. We offer customized pricing plans based on your specific requirements.

Can you help with publishing the app to the App Store and Google Play?

Yes, we handle the entire deployment process, including App Store and Google Play submissions, ensuring your app meets all guidelines for a smooth launch.

Will I own the app’s source code?

Yes, once the app is developed and the project is completed, you will have full ownership of the source code and intellectual property.

Can you upgrade an existing app?

Yes, we can enhance and upgrade your existing app with new features, improved performance, and design enhancements to keep it competitive.

Do you offer website maintenance services?

Yes, we offer comprehensive maintenance packages to ensure your website remains updated, secure, and performs optimally at all times.

How do you ensure data security and privacy in the apps?

We follow industry best practices and implement secure coding standards, data encryption, and compliance with relevant regulations like GDPR to ensure data security and privacy.

What industries do you specialize in for app development?

We cater to a variety of industries, including e-commerce, healthcare, education, entertainment, event management, and real estate, among others.
