Book Spot

"BookSpot" is a online bookstore application. This application is designed to allow users to browse, search, purchase, interact with books online, sell your used books, listen to audiobooks and can read book online. The user can do whatever they want to do with books.

Harmonize

This application helps musician to find fellow musicians to collaborate with them in their projects or events, they can rent instruments to recordings or events. And users can upload their works here and they can get the opportunity.

EduHub

A sleek and user-friendly edutech website UI designed for effortless navigation, enabling users to browse, purchase online courses, and earn certifications seamlessly. The interface emphasizes clarity, accessibility, and a modern design aesthetic to enhance the learning experience.

ShopNow

A comprehensive shop dashboard UI that provides officials with a clear overview of sales, profit, loss, and overall shop performance. Designed with intuitive visuals and analytics for efficient decision-making and tracking.

RecruitPro

This job recruiter's dashboard helps recruiters organize and manage job applicants efficiently. It provides a clear and quick overview of candidates, their progress, and the number of applications, making the hiring process faster and more organized, ultimately helping companies find the right people for their job openings.
